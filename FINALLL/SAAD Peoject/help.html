Test Accounts

Use these accounts to explore different roles and features:

  • student / studentpass
  • instructor / instructorpass
  • admin / adminpass
  • finance / financepass

Key Features

  • Course Registration: Students can register for up to 7 courses with automatic seat availability checks.
  • Payment Processing: Choose between Visa and Cash. Visa payments may be pending for finance verification.
  • Course Management: Instructors can create, edit, and delete courses with credit hours and seat counts.
  • Grade Tracking: Instructors enter grades, students view their performance.
  • Payment Verification: Finance staff approve or reject pending student payments.
  • Admin Reports: Administrators access enrollment summaries and system data.

Troubleshooting

  1. Redirected to login: Your session may have expired. Sign in again.
  2. Payment failed: Try again with Cash for guaranteed success, or wait for Finance approval.
  3. Course registration limited: You may have reached the 7 course maximum or the course is full.
  4. Reset demo data: Open DevTools, go to Application, find Local Storage, and remove the ums_data_v3 key.
