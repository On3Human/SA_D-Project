Glossary & Quick References

Terms, data stores and actor roles used across the prototype

Home · Prototype

Actors

  • Student: selects courses, pays fees, views grades.
  • Registrar/Instructor: manages courses, enters grades.
  • Finance/Admin: verifies payments, runs reports.

Key Data Stores

  • Student DB — profiles & enrollments.
  • Course DB — catalog and seats.
  • Grade DB — exam results & transcripts.
  • Finance DB — payments & receipts.
