University Management System

A modern platform for students to register for courses, manage payments, track grades, and for instructors to manage courses and assign grades. Finance teams verify payments, and administrators oversee the entire system.

Secure Access

Role-based login for students, instructors, administrators, and finance staff.

Easy Registration

Register for courses with automatic seat checks and flexible payment options.

Payment Tracking

Process payments securely and track pending transactions in real time.
